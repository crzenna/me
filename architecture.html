Architecture

Is it really a passion?

I am not one hudred percent sure if I should consider Architecture as a passion of mine.
I just know that when I go around and visit some place new, I am super affascinated by buildings and structures and, in general, the architecture.

I love how different architects have their own style, and how recognisable architectural styles can be

One of my favorite architect and architecture studio is Zaha Hadid and ZHA (Zaha Hadid Architects). I simply love how instantly recognizable their works are, and how well they fit into a landscape or in a neighboorhood.

Instagram

I did not study architecture, I do not have any technical foundations abut it. I just love the way it looks.
For this reason, I created an Instagram profile where I take what I think are interesting architectural alements, and turn them into graphic design element, turning them into icons/logos. And I think we can consider it as a passion of mine, digitally recreating architectural elements.
