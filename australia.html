Australia

Work

I was able to find a position as hotel receptionist during my first week in Sydney. I stayed in a hostel for the first ten days and then I moved to a shared apartment close to the place I was working. The working holiday visa (WHV) allows you to work for the same employes just for six months. For this reason, I got another job as receptionist in a different hotel starting on August 2018. I stayed there until the end of my stay in Australia. I never came back to Italy during the whole period abroad, and despite the fact that I missed my friends and family, I never wished to be elsewhere. In Australia I was happy.

People

I was surrounded by amazing people coming from all over the world.

Most of my time I was with Spanish people, with which I enjoyed many many barbecues by the ocean and crazy parties and festival.

The best part of my Australian experience was meeting people, exchanging ideas and information and travel tips and life tips.

Travels

During my 18 months of Australia I have been lucky enough to visit a lot of interesting places around the country and outside as well.

I travelled around Melbourne, Brisbane, Goldcoast, Byron Bay, Alice Springs, but also Bali in Indonesia, Singapore, Bangkok and Phucket in Thailand, and Manila and Palawan in the Philippines.

I love travelling, and Australia is in a very strategic position to do so.
The only thing I'm sorry for is not having had the chance to visit New Zeland!

Studies

Once my one year Visa expired I had two options: I could either go back home, or extend my stay by enrolling in a course and ask for a student visa.

Of course that's what I did

I enrolled in a 6 months Economics course and kept working part time enjoying the crazy weekends and memorable festival.

One day once I realised that I did not have enough time left there, I found myself sitting on the sofa and planning what would have been my next adventure in life: I applied to study at University in Edinburgh, UK.

I got accepted and after going back to Italy for two weeks I packed my suitcase again and flew to the UK
