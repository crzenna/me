University

Edinburgh Napier University is based around its three main Edinburgh campuses: Merchiston, Craiglockhart and Sighthill. It has over 19,500 students, including those on-campus in Scotland and others studying on transnational programmes abroad and online. In 2018 this included nearly 9,500 international and EU students, from more than 140 nations worldwide.

The course I am enrolled in is Web Design and Development, it's a four year course and it is part of the School of Computing

Work

When I first arrived I decided to go around the city and hand out my resumes to some hotels that were looking part time receptionists

I got hired the following week I arrived and started working at YOTEL Edinburgh every friday, staurday and sundau for a total of twenty hours per week

In this way I was able to afford my rent without having to ask additional money to my parents, and the fact that the hotel took me time only during weekends made possible that I could organise my studies and class attendancies.
